Dr. Agachai Sumalee is a faculty member at Chulalongkorn School of Integrated Innovation

His speciality is Inclusive Community or Smart City Innovation


Agachai Sumalee has a Ph.D. degree from University of Leeds, UK (Thesis: Optimal Road User Charging Scheme Design)

His work experience includes
Visiting Professor, Centre of Collaborative Research, Institute of Industrial Science, University of Tokyo, Japan
Visiting Fellow, Department of Urban Planning and Management, Kyoto University, Japan
Visiting Fellow, Institute for Transport Studies, University of Leeds, UK
Assistant Professor, Department of Civil and Structural Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University
Programme Leader, bachelor’s degree of Civil and Environmental Engineering, Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University
Associate Professor, Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University
Vice President, King Mongkut’s Institute of Technology Ladkrabang
Associate Professor, Director Smart City Research Centre (SCRC) King Mongkut’s Institute of Technology Ladkrabang
Advisor, Thailand Development Research Institute (TDRI)
and Professor, Hong Kong Polytechnic University
