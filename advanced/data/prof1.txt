
Professor Worsak Kanok-Nukulchai is the Executive Director of School of Integrated Innovation at Chulalongkorn University

His specialities are  Education Transformation, Innovative Technologies, Computational Methods, Finite Element Methods, Structural Engineering.

He has a Ph. D. at the University of California at Berkeley, 1978

Prior to working at the Chulalongkorn School of Integrated Innovation he worked at
Asian Institute of Technology and at the Diablo Nuclear Power Plant Project, Francisco, California, U.S.A.
